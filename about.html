About Me

I hear what others miss. After nearly a decade of practice, I've developed the ability to listen for the patterns that run beneath the surface of your words—the repetitions, contradictions, and revelations that most people never notice.

While other therapists offer techniques and coping strategies, I work with the deeper logic that drives your symptoms. I can show you why you repeat the same patterns, why certain situations trigger you in ways that surprise even yourself, and what your mind has been trying to communicate through the very problems that brought you here.

This insight didn't come from textbooks alone. My own experience of undertaking this type of work revealed how profound change happens—not through advice or techniques, but through understanding the hidden coordinates of your desire and the unconscious choices that shape your world.

I work exclusively online throughout Massachusetts, maintaining a carefully limited practice focused on people who are ready to discover what they don't yet know about themselves. Most who work with me have tried other approaches without lasting success, because the real answers lie deeper than conscious awareness.

Why Others Haven't Helped

Most therapy stays on the surface because most therapists don't know how to listen for what's really being said. They hear your complaints, your goals, your conscious intentions. I hear the contradictions, the slips, the places where your unconscious reveals itself despite your best efforts to remain in control.

The symptoms that brought you here aren't random. They follow a precise logic—one that becomes clear when you know how to look. Your repetitive patterns, your seemingly irrational fears, even your self-sabotage serve specific purposes that your conscious mind cannot access.

I can read these patterns because I understand how the unconscious operates. Where you see chaos and confusion, I see the underlying structure that organizes your experience. This is why transformation becomes possible in ways that surprise even longtime therapy veterans.

Preliminary Interviews

Our first few meetings allow me to assess whether you're ready for this depth of work. I'll listen for the specific ways your unconscious communicates and determine if this approach can access what's been driving your patterns.

Not everyone is ready for this work, and not every situation calls for it. But when the conditions are right, the changes that become possible often exceed what you thought was achievable.

What I Offer That Others Cannot

I have specialized training in understanding unconscious communication—the language of symptoms, dreams, slips of the tongue, and the meaningful coincidences that most dismiss as random. This allows me to work with the actual source of your difficulties rather than just managing their effects.

My practice is intentionally small because this work requires complete attention to the unique ways each person's unconscious expresses itself. I don't use standardized techniques because your unconscious doesn't operate according to standard formulas.

What This Means for You

If you're here, something has been trying to get your attention—through symptoms, through patterns, through a persistent sense that something needs to change. That "something" has intelligence and purpose, even if it currently feels like an obstacle.

I can help you understand what it's trying to communicate and why it's been necessary until now. From that understanding, genuine transformation becomes not just possible, but inevitable.
