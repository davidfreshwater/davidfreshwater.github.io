David M. Freshwater, LMHC

Licensed Mental Health Counselor

I bring nearly a decade of experience practicing Lacanian psychoanalysis. My approach draws from the understanding that the unconscious operates according to its own sophisticated logic—one that often makes more sense than our conscious attempts to manage our lives.

I work exclusively with adults who are ready for genuine change, not just symptom management. This isn't therapy focused on coping strategies, but an investigation that can fundamentally transform how you experience yourself and your world.

I came to this work through my own transformative experience of analysis, which continues to inform my practice. The courage it takes to begin this journey is something I deeply respect in each person who reaches out.

All sessions are conducted online via secure video or phone connection, allowing for flexibility while maintaining the intimacy necessary for analytic work. My practice is small and intentionally limited, focusing on ongoing psychoanalytic relationships.

My Approach to Practice

Unlike therapists who offer techniques and strategies, I create conditions where your unconscious wisdom can emerge. This requires a particular kind of listening—one attuned to the gaps, repetitions, and surprises in your speech.

I maintain a position of "not-knowing" that allows your unique truth to unfold. This doesn't mean I'm passive; rather, I actively engage with your material in ways that open new pathways for understanding.

My commitment is to the process of analysis itself—to maintaining the frame within which transformation becomes possible. This means respecting the power of the unconscious and trusting in its capacity to guide us toward what needs to be known.

The Initial Consultation

We begin with an initial consultation (roughly 1 hour) to explore whether analytic work makes sense for your particular situation. This isn't an evaluation—it's an opportunity to experience what it might be like to work together.

During this time, you'll have a chance to speak about what brings you to seek analysis, and I'll listen in the particular way that characterizes this work. By the end, we'll both have a sense of whether to proceed.

Working Together

My practice operates on Lacanian principles, which means sessions vary in length based on the logic of the unconscious rather than the clock. This approach respects the natural rhythms of analytic work.

I work with a limited number of analysands to ensure each person receives the focused attention this work requires. My practice is fully online, serving adults throughout Massachusetts who are ready to engage deeply with their unconscious material.

What to Expect

Initially, anxiety may increase as we approach what your psyche has kept hidden. This is actually a positive sign—it means we're getting close to material that matters. Many find that what seemed like setbacks were actually breakthroughs in disguise.

Unlike quick-fix approaches, this work creates lasting change. The investment—both financial and emotional—pays dividends for the rest of your life.
