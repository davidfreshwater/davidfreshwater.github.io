How This Work Happens

The same fight surfaces in every relationship, wearing different masks but carrying the same wounds. You make inexplicable mistakes just as the promotion becomes real. Joy feels dangerous—anxiety rises whenever happiness arrives. Stomach pains appear before every family gathering. You stumble over your own name while introducing yourself to someone who matters. You spill wine on your mother-in-law's white carpet for the second time this year. You've felt depressed for years, but you don't know why because you had a perfectly normal childhood. In dreams, you speed through busy city streets, but the brakes don't work.

These aren't random events.

They're your mind's way of insisting on being heard.
◦

How Sessions Work

You speak whatever comes to mind without censoring yourself. I listen for patterns, repetitions, contradictions, and the places where your words reveal more than you intended. A single slip might unlock everything.

Rather than managing symptoms or teaching coping strategies, this work reveals the hidden logic that organizes your experience. We discover why certain situations trigger you, why you repeat patterns that no longer serve you, and what your symptoms are trying to communicate.

We return again and again to certain phrases, forgotten moments, seemingly trivial details: a recurring number, a childhood smell, a phrase you can't stop saying. This continues until they lose their power over you and genuine change becomes possible.

1

You speak freely

Dreams, memories, current events, seemingly random thoughts are all welcome. What you dismiss as meaningless often contains the key to everything.

2

I listen for what you can't hear

The gaps in your speech, contradictions in your stories, words that appear again and again. What you don't say often matters as much as what you do.

3

Sessions follow natural rhythms

Unlike traditional 50-minute hours, sessions end when something essential has been articulated. This might be 15 minutes or 55. Your mind doesn't follow clock time.

4

Meaning emerges naturally

Rather than offering solutions, I respond in ways that help you hear your own words differently, allowing meanings you didn't know were there to surface.

What Happens in Sessions

You'll be invited to speak freely to follow your thoughts wherever they lead. This might feel unusual if you're used to structured conversations, but most people discover they know more than they realized.

I'll intervene at specific moments with a question, by highlighting a word you've used, or by ending when something essential has been revealed. These interventions open new paths for exploration.

There's no "right" way to do this work. Some people explore dreams, others daily life, others memories or fantasies. What matters is speaking freely and staying curious about what emerges.

"Little by little, from within your own words, another meaning forms and surprises you, then falls apart, taking the pain with it."

The Process of Change

Initially, anxiety may increase as we approach what your mind has kept hidden. This signals we're getting close to material that matters. Apparent setbacks often prove to be breakthroughs in disguise.

Over time, you will experience:

Dreams Clarify

Your dreams become more vivid and meaningful as deeper patterns emerge

Connections Emerge

Unexpected links between life experiences suddenly make perfect sense

Symptoms Transform

What once tormented you shifts into something you can understand

Patterns Break

You stop repeating cycles without forcing yourself to change

Relief Arrives

Long-carried weight finally finds words and begins to lift away

Confidence Grows

A quiet certainty emerges that no longer depends on others

The part of you that you're not aware of resists being known directly. It reveals itself through dreams, mistakes, symptoms, and slips of the tongue. Our work creates conditions where these communications can be heard and transformed.

The Relationship

This isn't a relationship between equals sharing experiences. I occupy a specific position that allows the hidden parts of your mind to emerge. You will develop strong feelings about me, both positive and negative. These reactions become crucial material for understanding your patterns with others.

All sessions are conducted online via secure video or phone connection. This format has proven remarkably effective, as the deeper parts of your mind speak just as clearly through digital connection.

Your Investment

This depth of work requires genuine commitment: temporal, financial, and emotional. Your investment reflects and enables the profound shifts possible through engaging with what lies beneath conscious awareness.

Fees are discussed during our preliminary interviews, as the investment in this work is an important part of the process itself.

Duration and Commitment

The work continues until you've experienced sufficient change, until the repetitive cycles that brought you here have transformed into new possibilities for living.

This approach serves those ready to discover something unexpected about themselves. The people who benefit most have reached a point where staying the same feels more dangerous than changing.
