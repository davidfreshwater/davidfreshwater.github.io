How This Work Happens

You call your new partner by your ex's name at a dinner with friends. Stomach pain appears before every family gathering. You forget important meetings despite having them in your calendar. Every July, depression arrives like clockwork, though nothing happened in July that you can recall.

These aren't random events—they're your mind's way of insisting on being heard.

"What you don't know about yourself is precisely what runs your life."
◦

How Sessions Work

You speak whatever comes to mind without censoring yourself. I listen for patterns, repetitions, contradictions, and the places where your words reveal more than you intended. What seems irrelevant often proves most revealing.

Unlike approaches that strengthen your defenses or provide coping strategies, this work reveals the hidden logic that organizes your experience. We discover why certain situations trigger you, why you repeat patterns that no longer serve you, and what your symptoms are trying to communicate.

We return again and again to certain phrases, forgotten moments, seemingly trivial details—a recurring number, a childhood smell, a phrase you can't stop saying—until they lose their power over you and genuine change becomes possible.

1

You speak freely

Dreams, memories, current events, seemingly random thoughts—all are welcome. What you dismiss as meaningless often contains the key to everything.

2

I listen for what you can't hear

Patterns, contradictions, slips of the tongue, and the gaps in your speech. What you don't say often matters as much as what you do.

3

Sessions follow natural rhythms

Unlike traditional 50-minute hours, sessions end when something essential has been articulated. This might be 15 minutes or 55—your unconscious doesn't follow clock time.

4

Meaning emerges naturally

Rather than offering solutions, I respond in ways that help you hear your own words differently—allowing meanings you didn't know were there to surface.

What Happens in Sessions

You'll be invited to speak freely—to follow your thoughts wherever they lead. This might feel unusual at first, especially if you're used to more structured conversations. Most people discover they know more than they realized.

I'll intervene at specific moments—sometimes with a question, sometimes by highlighting a word or phrase you've used, sometimes by ending the session when something essential has been revealed. These interventions open new avenues for exploration rather than providing answers.

There's no "right" way to do this work. Some people talk about dreams, others about daily life, others about memories or fantasies. What matters is that you speak as freely as possible and remain curious about what emerges.

"Little by little, from within your own words, another meaning forms and surprises you, then falls apart, taking the pain with it."
◦

The Process of Change

Initially, anxiety may increase as we approach what your mind has kept hidden. This is actually a positive sign—it means we're getting close to material that matters. What seemed like setbacks often prove to be breakthroughs in disguise.

Over time, you will experience:

Dreams Become Meaningful

Dreams become more vivid and revealing as your unconscious finds its voice

Connections Emerge

Unexpected links between disparate parts of your life suddenly make sense

Symptoms Transform

A fundamental shift in how you experience what once tormented you

Relief Arrives

The surprising lightness when long-carried weight finally finds words

Patterns Break

You stop repeating the same cycles without forcing yourself to change

Confidence Emerges

A quiet certainty about who you are that no longer depends on others' approval

Your unconscious resists being known directly. It reveals itself through dreams, mistakes, symptoms, and slips of the tongue. Our work creates conditions where these communications can be heard and transformed.

The Relationship

This isn't a relationship between equals sharing experiences. I occupy a specific position that allows your unconscious to emerge. You will develop strong feelings about me—positive and negative. These feelings become crucial material for understanding your patterns with others.

All sessions are conducted online via secure video or phone connection. The online format has proven remarkably effective for this work, as your unconscious speaks just as clearly through digital connection.

Your Investment

This depth of work requires genuine commitment—temporal, financial, and emotional. Your investment reflects and enables the profound shifts possible through engaging with your unconscious.

Fees are discussed during our preliminary interviews, as the investment in this work is an important part of the process itself.

Duration and Commitment

Some work lasts months, others years. The work continues until you've experienced sufficient change—until the repetitive cycles that brought you here have given way to new possibilities for living.

This approach serves those who are ready to discover something unexpected about themselves and who understand that authentic change requires patient engagement with what lies beneath conscious awareness. The people who benefit most are those who've reached a point where staying the same feels more dangerous than changing.
