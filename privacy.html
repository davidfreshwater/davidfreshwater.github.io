Our Commitment to Your Privacy

The relationship between analyst and analysand rests on a foundation of absolute confidentiality. This privacy policy outlines how we protect not just your personal information, but the sacred space of analysis itself.

The Analytic Frame

Everything spoken in our sessions remains strictly confidential. This confidentiality extends beyond legal requirementsâ€”it is an ethical commitment fundamental to psychoanalytic work. The only exceptions to confidentiality are those required by Massachusetts law:

  • Imminent risk of harm to yourself or others
  • Suspected abuse of a child, elderly person, or disabled individual
  • Court order requiring disclosure

Should any of these situations arise, I would discuss with you what information must be shared and with whom, to the extent possible.

Digital Security & HIPAA Compliance

All electronic communications employ bank-level encryption that exceeds HIPAA requirements:

  • Video Sessions: Conducted through HIPAA-compliant platforms with end-to-end encryption
  • Email Communications: Encrypted email available upon request for sensitive communications
  • Record Storage: Any clinical notes are stored in HIPAA-compliant systems with 256-bit encryption
  • Payment Processing: Secure, PCI-compliant payment systems that never store full credit card information

Your Rights Under HIPAA

As my client, you have specific rights regarding your health information:

  • Right to request restrictions on uses and disclosures of your information
  • Right to request confidential communications
  • Right to inspect and copy your clinical record (though in psychoanalysis, this is rarely therapeutically indicated)
  • Right to request amendments to your record
  • Right to receive an accounting of disclosures

Online Presence & Discovery

I maintain clear boundaries regarding online interactions:

  • I do not accept friend or contact requests from current or former clients on social media
  • I do not search for clients online unless there is a compelling clinical reason, which would be discussed with you
  • Any testimonials or case examples on this website are completely anonymized composites

Record Keeping

In keeping with psychoanalytic tradition and Massachusetts law, I maintain minimal records focused on:

  • Dates of sessions
  • Fee arrangements
  • Any formal diagnoses (if required for billing)
  • Essential contact information

Detailed process notes, if kept, remain separate from your official record and are used only to enhance our work together.

Questions or Concerns

If you have any questions about this privacy policy or how your information is protected, please don't hesitate to discuss them with me. The trust required for analytic work demands complete transparency about these matters.
