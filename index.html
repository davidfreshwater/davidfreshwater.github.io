How I Work

Whether this is your first time considering therapy or you've tried other approaches, you're here because something important is no longer working and you need help. You may have already tried coaching, medication, mindfulness, willpower and discipline, and various alphabet-soup therapies (CBT, DBT, ACT, IFS, EMDR). And yet you're still stuck in the same loops.

That's because most approaches focus on managing symptoms rather than understanding what drives them. I work differently—transformation happens through your own words, as new meanings emerge and old patterns naturally dissolve.

I often work with people who've tried other approaches but didn't find lasting change because the deeper patterns were never addressed. Your mind communicates through symptoms, dreams, everyday mistakes, and slips of the tongue. I create conditions where you can begin to hear what you've been trying to tell yourself all along.

All sessions are conducted online or over the phone and are completely confidential, providing a private space where you can explore without fear of judgment or exposure.
