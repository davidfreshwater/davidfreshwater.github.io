Next Steps

1

Reach Out

Contact me by email or phone to express your interest in beginning analysis. Share a bit about what brings you to seek this work.

2

Schedule Consultation

We'll arrange a consultation (roughly 1 hour) at a time that works for both of us. This initial session allows for genuine exploration.

3

Experience the Work

During the consultation, you'll have space to speak freely while I listen in the particular way that characterizes analytic work.

4

Decide Together

By the end of our consultation, we'll both have a sense of whether to proceed with ongoing analysis.
